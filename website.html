Ephemeral Queues: A globally available ephemeral messaging system.

Use cases:

Pass data from one place to another in a ordered way.

Details:

There are a total of 100,000 possible queues. Queues are purged and deleted every 24 hours at 10 AM ET.

Ephemeral Queues only accept signed int32 values.

Once data is consumed it is deleted forever.

API:

  • POST /queue -> queueId
  • GET /polling/:id/ -> nextVal
  • POST /queue/:id/addition -> null
  • DELETE /queue/:id -> null
